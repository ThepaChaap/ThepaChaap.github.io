Recipe #1 - Paneer Makhanwala

  • Time: 30 mins
  • Servings: 6
Ingredients Quantity
Chilli Powder 1 teaspoon
Paneer 500 gm
Onion 3-4
Tomatoes 400 gm
Butter 6 tbsp
Garam Masala 1 teaspoon
Fresh Creme 400 gm
Green Chilli 4
Dried Fenugreek Leaves 4 tbsp
Salt As per requirement

Step 1: To start with, wash paneer and chop it properly into small cubes on a chopping board. Chop the green chilies as well. Take a pan and keep it on medium flame. And butter to the pan and let it melt, it won't take much time to melt.

Step 2: After the butter melts, add green chilies, tomato puree, kasuri methi, salt, red chili powder to the pan. Stir well to let the ingredients mix.

Step 3: Cover the pan or you can also keep it uncovered and simmer for 7-8 minutes by reducing the flame to low or otherwise your spices can burn.

Step 4: Then add cream and garam masala to the pan. Let the mixture cook for another 5 minutes on medium flame. Once the mixture is cooked properly, finally add paneer cubes to the pan and cook for another 2 minutes.

Step 5: Take out the paneer curry in a bowl and garnish with coriander. Serve hot with chapati and rice.

Recipe #2 - Oreo Cake

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Servings: 5
Ingredients Quantity
Oreo 12 cookies
Powdered Sugar 1 teaspoon
Baking Powder 1/2 teaspoon
Milk 1 cup

Step 1: In a blender, crush the Oreo cookies to a fine powder. Now, add milk, sugar, baking powder and blend well. Adjust the quantity of milk in a way it is of pouring consistency.

Step 2: Now, take a microwave-safe bowl and pour the mixture on it. Microwave on high for 4-5 minutes or till a knife inserted in the middle of the cake, comes out clean. Gradually increase the baking time until the cake bakes perfectly.

Step 3: Take it out of the dish carefully, place on a platter and pour chocolate ganache, whipped cream or garnish with chopped nuts.

Recipe #3 - Rava Cake

  • Time: 30 mins
  • Servings: 6
Ingredients Quantity
Semolina 1/2 cup
Ghee 1/2 cup
Milk 2 cups
Sugar 2 1/2 cups

Step 1: To begin with, place a pan on medium low flame. Pour ghee in it and heat it. Once heated, add milk and semolina (rava) along with sugar and let simmer for about 10 minutes or until well-cooked.

Step 2:Then, transfer in a serving dish and put the same in the refrigerator for about 20 minutes to chill. Once done, take out and cut into small bite sized pieces.
